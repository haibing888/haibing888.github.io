English-Hebrew Map

The Hebrew language map was the toughest to create. Strictly speaking, the Hebrew language does not fit well on a map. Verbs are conjugated according to gender, number, time, and a definite article, which makes presentation on a map all the more difficult. Consequently, we spent more time on the design of the map than the actual content. It was important that the color scheme, navigation, signs and structure are all straightforward, avoiding any mix up for users. Still, using the map will get you 100% correct sentences as long as you follow the lingual pattern.
